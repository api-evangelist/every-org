Set up in seconds for over 1M+ nonprofits.</li><li><strong>Pricing:</strong> No set-up or platform fees. The donations are made to Every.org which in turn grants nonprofits in cash, similar to a DAF. Nonprofits never take custody of the crypto and only has to interact with cash.</p><p>The Giving Block, Engiven, and other for-profit options are a payments processor that handle the technical challenges but the nonprofit will be taking on the risks involved.
